Tags: #dj

After watching so many Netflix serieses I got an Idea to imagine myself like a character in series while doing mundane but important work like cleaning, cooking, exercising etc. All the leading characters do these things gracefully and I watch them. Why not imagine doing great. Also from flow book how you treat the task determines how you will feel, even slaves are happy, kings are unhappy, so better to control the thoughts.

\
e.g I don’t sleep on time. Imagine I’m a hero who sleeps on time, enjoys the feeling of sleeping.
