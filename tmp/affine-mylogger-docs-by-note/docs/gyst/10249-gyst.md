# Note 10249

Tags: #gyst

Under Promise, Over Deliver
Ask questions
  I’ve developed a list of questions that I ask all of our new clients: 
  What’s your company’s story? 
  What are the biggest objections of your prospective customers? 
  What is the specific action that you want a viewer to take after viewing your video?
Do Something You’re Willing to Stay Up Late For
Choose a Growing Industry. As it’s new less competitor and customers are growing.
You’re only as Good as Your Portfolio. Impressive and Diverse portfolio.
Get real feedback.
Ask for Endorsements
  What would you tell a friend who’s thinking about employing our services?
You’re Always Looking for New Clients. Even if you have work in hand for 6 months, keep looking for new clients.
Don’t Expect to Earn a Full-Time Income Right Away
Nothing’s More Important than the Quality of Your Work
  Initially keep doing even if work is small. You don’t know who is going to see your work.
Recurring Work
