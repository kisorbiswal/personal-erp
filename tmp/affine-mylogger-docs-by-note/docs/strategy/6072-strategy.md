# Note 6072

Tags: #strategy

Focused companies sometimes can develop new products more rapidly than larger firms because of the conflicts and distractions that broad scope often creates. The theory of disruption suggests, however, that once
they have developed and established the viability of their superior product, entrepreneurs who
have entered on a sustaining trajectory should turn around and sell out to one of the industry
leaders behind them.
