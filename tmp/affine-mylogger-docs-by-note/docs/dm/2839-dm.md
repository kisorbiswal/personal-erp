# Note 2839

Tags: #dm

People 
frequently use machine learning techniques to gain insight into the structure of their 
data rather than to make predictions for new cases. In fact, a prominent and successful 
line of research in machine learning began as an attempt to compress a huge 
database of possible chess endgames and their outcomes into a data structure of 
reasonable size.
