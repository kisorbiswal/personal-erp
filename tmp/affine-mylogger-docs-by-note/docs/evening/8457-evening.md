# Note 8457

Tags: #evening

Either write something worth reading or do something worth writing.                                                                                                     — Benjamin FranklinIf your primary business is to create something, then focus on creating something worth writing. Don’t get misled by the content marketing experts, who either does not know why it works for them or intentionally concealing the fact why it works for them. The people who profess about content marketing are running businesses where primary objective is to teach about something(especially marketing), to teach anything you need great content. They are, undoubtedly, creating great content. Here they are creating something that is their primary focus, this works great for them. But rest of us, whose primary focus is to create something else, are trapped to think writing content is the only way of doing marketing in current times. I’m not against educating the customers or anyone for that matter, but against it deviates you from your primary job. If you are building an accounting software, you don’t get a better software by writing introductory content about marketing. Let your customers learn from already available sources, redirect them there!
