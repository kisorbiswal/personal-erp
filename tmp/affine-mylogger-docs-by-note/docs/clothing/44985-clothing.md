# Note 44985

Tags: #clothing

Shape wear, skin colour is better
Wear fitting clothes, not too tight. Tight around chest and arms is okay, but must be lose around belly(mid section)
Stretch out the mid area of cloth
Dark colours, especially black
Low contrast- If wearing a light colour pant, wear a light colour shirt too
Not need to be mono chromatic. Wear dark to dark, blue to blue
Do not tuck in
