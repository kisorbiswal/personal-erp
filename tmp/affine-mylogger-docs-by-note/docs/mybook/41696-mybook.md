# Note 41696

Tags: #mybook

This book throws light on the inner workings of Children's brain. Useful guide for parents and teachers.

Generalists are leading the innovation space in the age of super speciality.

General truths, when seen and understood, are so much more powerful instruments than are mere particular and detached bits of knowledge, that surrender upon this point will hardly be permitted by any able teacher.

The interest in these general truths is, in a sense, less natural, more a matter of civilization, and has to be imposed upon the child by a kind of contagious interest felt by another who can see the endless applications of what is universal.
