# Note 8290

Tags: #inb

TAKING YOUR SALES PROCESS INBOUND
60% of customer have made the decision before talking to Sales Rep.
Lead intelligence: How they interacted? Revenue, size etc.
Create trust with research. Try to understand what their goals are. 
Speak the prospect's language.
Be helpful
Have a common friend, customer, following same person in twitter.
Talk as if the prospect is your best friend. Set the context why are you calling?

If no response send voice mail, followed by email.
