ZOPA=  Zone of potential agreement

Tips and Tricks
Type of opponent, Interest based or Bargaining 

Anchoring, Anchor High so we will have space to negotiate

Make the first offer =  Anchor
Counter anchor, make it realistic but still high anchor

BATNA= Best alternative to a negotiation agreement, Never go below this. Avoid bad agreement being emotional

Avoid range offers, the opponent may go below the lower range.

Do not make concession in a row, let the opponent make a concession.

Calculated concession, each one smaller than the previous one.

Trading concession, when making an concession ask something in return. e.g When I ask something for lower cost, they show a smaller item.
 Make multiple packages, if I make this concession, I should get that.(Use their interests here)
 
 On making concession, give both the concession and condition in same sentence. Otherwise the opponent will say you have already agreed.
