Tags: #req

Job Description  Software Development Engineer Search Technology
 A9 Bangalore Search team is looking for Software Development Engineers  for building cutting edge search technologies for use by individuals and  businesses of all sizes.
 The A9 Bangalore team is the India development arm of A9.com, the search and advertising technology subsidiary of Amazon.com.
 If you have a solid understanding of the fundamental algorithms, system  design and are able to produce bullet-proof code, then we are looking  for you. We want someone who can explore challenging new areas and build  high-quality software solutions for new and existing products.
 
 As a Software Development Engineer, you will:
 Design, develop and support a world-class search platform serving individuals and businesses of all sizesDesign,  develop and support crawlers and extractors for various data stores;  build client facing utilities to make adoption of search platform a  breeze for customersHave a relentless focus on scalability,  latency, performance, robustness and cost trade-offs -- especially those  present in highly virtualized, elastic, cloud-based environmentsWhat you need:
 A  solid understanding of fundamental algorithms, system design, and the  ability to reason through the myriad trade-offs that are present in  large distributed systemsThe ability to produce bullet-proof  code that is fault-tolerant, efficient and maintainable; our primary  languages are Java and PythonA relentless focus on customers satisfactionA  strong intellectual curiosity to continually challenge what exists and  explore what should be changed to best meet evolving business and market  needsBachelors, Masters or Ph.D. (Masters/Ph.D. preferred) in  Computer Science or equivalent; At least five years of Software  Development experience.You are going to love this job because you will:
