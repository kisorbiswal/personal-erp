Tags: #req

Google: Minimum Qualifications

BS in Computer Science or related field or equivalent practical experience.   1 year relevant industry work experience.Experience programming in C/C++ and/or Java.

Master's or PhD in Computer Science or related field.Solid foundation in computer science, with strong competencies in data structures, algorithms and software design.Experience with large systems software design and development, with extensive knowledge of UNIX/Linux.Significant development software with strong knowledge of C/C++ and/or Java.Coding skills in Python or JavaScript/AJAX, database design and SQL, and/or knowledge of TCP/IP and network programming.
