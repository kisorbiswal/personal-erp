There are 5 Parts of Every Business, each of which flows into the next:\n 1 Value Creation - Discovering what people need or want, then creating it.\n 2 Marketing - Attracting attention and building demand for what you’ve created.\n 3 Sales - Turning prospective customers into paying customers.\n 4 Value Delivery - Giving your customers what you’ve promised and ensuring that they’re satisfied.\n 5 Finance - Bringing in enough money to keep going and make your effort worthwhile.
