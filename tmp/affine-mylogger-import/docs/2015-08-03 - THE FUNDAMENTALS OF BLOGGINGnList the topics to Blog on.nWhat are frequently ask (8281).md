THE FUNDAMENTALS OF BLOGGINGnList the topics to Blog on.nWhat are frequently asked questions?nWhat are the keywords?nEach post address one specific question, not mix all.nCrete working topic, narrow down to specific info, a topic may be of multiple posts.nShorten the title.(50-60 char)nSet clear expectation in the title.nUse provocative language.nUse white spaces, so text does not look cluttered even in a long post.nUser section headers.nNumbered points.nBold important text.nPost one image loosely related image on top of each Blog post.nOptimize the post around the long tail keyword.nUse long tailed keyword naturally in body, but don't repeat. Can use synonyms.nKeywords does not need to appear in same order.nLink to citation when available.nStrategically offer next step to convert to lead.nGive people to option to subscribe.nPut a Call to Action in each post. May be more info to read, what's next.nLink to internal posts.nShould have a plan and schedule of posts.nnAnalyse: By Topic, Author, Promotion channel.nnnPromote them: Twitter, Linkedin, FB,
